# 📖 MangaPlus Sorted Stream Downloader

A lightweight userscript that automatically captures, sorts, and downloads MangaPlus chapter pages directly in high-quality **PNG** format.

---

## ⚡ Features
- **Auto-Sorting**: Automatically detects and arranges pages sequentially from top to bottom.
- **Canvas Conversion**: Converts web viewer canvases into clean, ready-to-use PNG images.
- **Convenient UI**: Features a minimal overlay control panel to easily start and pause scanning.

---

## 🚀 How to Use (Tutorial)

1. **Prepare the Viewer**: Open your desired chapter on MangaPlus and switch the reading layout to **Vertical Mode**.
2. **Pre-load Pages**: Scroll all the way down to the bottom of the page to ensure every image and canvas fully loads.
3. **Download**: Click the **"Enable Auto-Scan"** button on the control panel to automatically fetch and download all pages sequentially.

> [!NOTE]  
> Sometimes fewer files may download than the page counter on the site displays. This is a known MangaPlus behavior (due to how they segment images), but rest assured, your downloaded pages will be fully complete!

---

## ⚙️ Installation

1. Install the [Tampermonkey](https://tampermonkey.net) extension for your browser.
2. Click on your published script link on **[Greasy Fork](https://greasyfork.org/ru/scripts/584172-mangaplus-sorted-stream-downloader-png)** (or open your `script.user.js` file directly) and click **Install**.
